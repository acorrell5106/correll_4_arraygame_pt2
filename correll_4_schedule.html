What Are The Classes I Have To Take?

  1. Class 1: World Studies - This class is a hybrid of English and World Geography, making the work twice as difficult. I am not the best when it comes to Geopgraphy, especially World Geography (I barely knew where a fourth of our states were located), but I'm decent when it comes to English. Sadly, I'm not a huge fan of either class, so it won't be gaining a stop in my list of favorite classes any time soon.
  2. Class 2: Orchestra - I have been playing the violin for about four years now, so this class was essential in my Freshman year of High School. I really enjoy the class, as well as our instructor, and I'm glad that I still get to be taught something new everyday, despite four years already under my belt.
  3. Class 3: German I - I wasn't quiet sure what language I was going to take in High School, mainly because there were no languages that I had a reason to know, besides just knowing them. I felt that German would seem the most interesting, yet I heard rumors of how difficult learning German could be. However, I am very glad I picked German because I have an amazing teacher who does a very good job of making sure we understand, so I am never lost. I am looking forward to continuing the class next year as well.
  4. Class 4: Computer Science Pre-AP - This was the class I was most excited to take this year, mainly because my dream is to become either a software developer or game developer. I wasn't dissapointed with what I recieved in this class, such as how to problem solve and how to work with the command line. I do get rather confused at times, but I try and keep working, because I know that I will get there.
  5. Class 5: Web Design - This class was an unexpected treat, considering that we were only supposed to have one elective. However, I managed to gain my health and most of my PE credits during the summer, so I elected to take a new DATA class. I wasn't sure if I was going to enjoy this class, but it ended up being one of my favorite classes. I love being able to customize my own website and I still am able to problem solve.
  6. Class 6: Geometry Pre-AP - I have never been good at math (maybe in third grade), so I was pretty nervous coming to this class. However, I learned that I am much better at Geometry than I ever was at Algebra, so the class so far is pretty decent.
  7. Class 7: Physics Pre-AP - In all my years of school, Science was my favorite class to take, mainly because it was the most interesting. However, I have always hated Physics, since it's basically math on steroids. I am almost always confused, and when I do understand something, we have already moved on to the next thing. My teacher is a bit strict, but he does try and help us understand, even if it does fly over my head once in a while. I'm just glad that I can end the day with this class so I don't have to think about it for the rest of my day.
