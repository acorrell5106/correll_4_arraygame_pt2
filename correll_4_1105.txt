Addison Correll's Journal 1105

	What are strings?
Strings are lines of code, words and letters exclusively.
	What are they good for?
Strings are good for writing out sentences that do not involve numbers, perhaps if you are printing out a name or a saying.
	What are some string methods?
Some string methods would be Compare, Equals, ToLower, and ToUpper, and these will identify, compare, or change a string that is specified by the method.
	How do we identify string methods?
To identify a string method, you would find a dot next to it.
