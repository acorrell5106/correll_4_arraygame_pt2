Hobbies

What Do I Like To Do, Besides Petting Cats?

Well, this is a pretty difficult question to answer. I mean, I could spend the rest of my life just petting cats! However, to save my sanity, as well as to not get my hands scratched to ribbons, I had to find some other hobbies to invest my time in. One of the things that I enjoy doing is playing the violin. The violin almost sounds like a horde of cats if you think about it, so I always try to play whenever I can. Another thing I enjoy doing is reading. There are times where you can't be surrounded by cats, such as the anytime that I am not in my house, so my sanity tends to run thin in these situations. However, I found that escaping into a book can fill this gaping hole, for a short while anyway, and I can even read to my cats if I so choose. Finally, I enjoy playing video games. Like books, video games offer a new realm to explore (even if most of these realms don't have cats) and this is my go-to entertainment source at home, especially when there are no felines in the vicinity (if you couldn't already tell, I really like cats).
