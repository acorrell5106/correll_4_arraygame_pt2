History

The Documentary of Me

My name is Addison Correll and I am 14 years old. I was born on February 21, even though I was supposed to be born on Valentine's Day. However, February 21 is national Sherlock Holmes day, which I think is pretty cool. I was born here in San Antonio, TX, and I have only moved once in my entire life, and the house was only about 100 feet away from my previous abode. I guess this is how I became a homebody, and definently an introvert, because all I want to do is spend all day at home. However, I somehow managed to be social in situations that called for it, such as school. I attended Windcrest Elementary School, and this is where I started realizing that my education was much more important to me than being the most popular kid in school. I felt that the classes I were taking were slow, until I was introduced to GT and their classes. I joined GT in 1st grade, and I have never left since. I knew that I wanted to have the same challenge for as long as possible to ensure the most out of school. I then joined Kreuger Middle School's KSAT program, making new friends and improving my skills. I, again, knew I wanted a challenge, so I joined DATA at Roosevelt High School. I don't know why I enjoy challenges, since I have pretty bad anxiety issues, but I just could never stand normal school, normal classes. I like to know that I can ensure my future career now that I have taken on these challenges already. That's pretty much my history, when it comes to the important things. However, I do have two cats called Bubby and Jill, although I call them Bubble Butt and Jilly Bean, respectively. I have never owned a dog, but if I did I would love to have an Irish Setter. My favorite food is brownies, and I am pretty sure that will never change. Finally, the last interseting thing about me is my initials, which are A.B.C. Not really that special, but I thought you should know.
