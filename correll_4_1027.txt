Addison Correll's Journal 1027

	What is the process of creativity?
The process of creativity is first having some kind of inspiration, and then being able to brainstorm ways of changing that idea into one that has your own unique touch.
	What are the specific steps involved?
The specific steps involved in creativity is:
1) Find inspiration
2) Find more inspiration
3) Brainstorm for as long as you need
4) Come up with a new idea based on the inspiration you gathered
5) Make your new idea unique, make it you!
